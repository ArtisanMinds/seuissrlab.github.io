Welcome to the Intelligent Small-Scale Robot Laboratory (ISSRLAB) at Southeast University. Directed by Chief Young Professor Qianqian Wang from the School of Mechanical Engineering, our lab is dedicated to pioneering research and innovation in the field of micro and small-scale intelligent robotics. We focus on the design, development, and integration of advanced robotic systems that aim to bridge the gap between theoretical research and real-world applications. Join us on a journey of discovery and exploration into the future of robotics technology.
